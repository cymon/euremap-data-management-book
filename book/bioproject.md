# 2. BioProject

The BioProject metadata accession was orginally an initiative of the [NIH NCBI](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/bioproject). In EMBL they are more generally referred to as a Study and receive a secondary accession number assigned by EMBL as well as the NCBI BioProject accession number.

Here is an example in the EMBL ENA database: [ERP193236](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/browser/view/ERP193236) and the same example in NCBI: [PRJEB111370](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/bioproject/?term=PRJEB111370)

## How register a BioProject/Study

- Sign in to the [ENA Webin Portal](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/submit/webin/login)
- Click "Register Study" in the "Studies (Projects)" panel
- Provide the Submission Details (Release date, Study Name, Short description,
  Abstract)


After submission your accession may take several days to propogate to the NCBI
databases, but should be immediately visible from the [EMBL Search](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/browser/search) if made
public.
