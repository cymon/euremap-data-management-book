# 3. ENA Webin Submission Portal

Through the EMBL Webin Submission Portal you can create BioProject/Study
accessions (named "Studies (Projects)" in the dashboard), BioSamples accessions
(named "Samples" on the dashboard), and submit sequence data to ENA (named "Raw
Reads (Experiments and Runs)" in the dashboard). 

- The EMBL Webin Submission Portal can be found here: [https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/submit/webin/login](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/submit/webin/login)
- Register if necessary
